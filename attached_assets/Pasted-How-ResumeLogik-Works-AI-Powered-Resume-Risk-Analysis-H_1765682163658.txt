How ResumeLogik Works

AI-Powered Resume Risk Analysis & Hiring Support

ResumeLogik is an AI-powered resume analysis and risk-prevention platform designed to help employers, recruiters, and agencies make more informed, consistent, and defensible hiring decisions — without replacing human judgment or performing background checks.

We do not make hiring decisions.
We do not verify employment or education.
We do not access external databases or third-party records.

ResumeLogik provides decision support, not decisions.

What ResumeLogik Actually Does

ResumeLogik analyzes only the information voluntarily provided by a candidate, typically in the form of a résumé or application content uploaded by the employer.

Using proprietary AI logic, the platform evaluates patterns, inconsistencies, and risk indicators commonly associated with résumé fraud, exaggeration, or misalignment — then presents those findings in a clear, structured format for human review.

Think of ResumeLogik as a pre-screening intelligence layer that helps you ask better questions — not an automated gatekeeper.

The AI Analysis Process (Step-by-Step)
1. Resume Content Intake

The system analyzes the résumé text exactly as submitted.
No enrichment. No verification. No external lookups.

2. Structural & Consistency Analysis

AI evaluates internal consistency across the document, including:

Name, contact, and formatting alignment

Timeline sequencing (gaps, overlaps, unrealistic progression)

Role and responsibility consistency

Education and credential plausibility

Skill alignment relative to stated roles

3. Authenticity & Risk Signal Detection

The platform flags potential risk signals such as:

Overly polished or AI-generated language patterns

Skill inflation or role mismatch

Copy-paste errors (wrong names, emails, mismatched companies)

Inconsistent job descriptions across time

Unrealistic career acceleration or compression

These are signals, not accusations.

4. Scoring & Categorization

Results are grouped into:

Low concern indicators

Moderate review indicators

High-attention risk indicators

Each flag includes plain-language explanations so hiring teams understand why something was flagged.

5. Human Review & Decision Support

You decide what matters.
You decide what to ignore.
You decide next steps.

ResumeLogik never auto-rejects candidates and never determines eligibility.

What ResumeLogik Does Not Do

To be explicit:

❌ No employment verification

❌ No education verification

❌ No criminal history checks

❌ No credit reports

❌ No identity validation

❌ No third-party data sources

❌ No hiring recommendations

Because ResumeLogik does not collect, verify, or report consumer data from external sources, it does not operate as a Consumer Reporting Agency (CRA).

Why ResumeLogik Is Not a CRA

ResumeLogik does not meet the definition of a consumer report under the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) because:

All analysis is derived solely from employer-submitted résumé content

No external databases or verification services are used

No factual determinations are made about a candidate’s background

No pass/fail or hire/no-hire recommendations are issued

The platform provides analytical insights, not factual findings.

ResumeLogik functions as a risk-prevention and decision-support tool, similar to résumé review checklists or internal screening heuristics — enhanced with AI for scale and consistency.
