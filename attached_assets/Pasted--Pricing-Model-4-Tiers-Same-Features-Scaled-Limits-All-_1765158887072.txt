The ONLY differences:

🟦 1. Free Tier — “Starter”

Price: $0 / month
Ideal For: Solopreneurs, micro-businesses, or first-time testers.

Limits:

1 active job

10 candidates per month

AI usage: 2 AI actions per candidate

(e.g., Run Resume Logic Check + Generate Interview Questions)

Purpose:

Let small businesses actually use your platform before committing.

Free users self-qualify → many convert to paid once they start actively hiring.

🟩 2. Eco Tier — “Eco”

Price: $29.99 / month
Ideal For: Small businesses hiring occasionally.

Limits:

3 active job positions

50 candidates per month

AI usage: 2 AI actions per candidate (same as all tiers)

Why it sells:

Most small companies only hire 2–5 times a year.

3 positions = good breathing room without overwhelming you with costs.

Perfect mid-range entry-tier for SMBs.

🟧 3. Pro Tier — “Pro”

Price: $49.99 / month
Ideal For: Growing companies, agencies, or businesses hiring regularly.

Limits:

10 active job positions

250 candidates per month

AI usage: 2 AI actions per candidate

Perks:

Higher active job cap fits companies with multiple roles (warehouse + office + seasonal hiring).

Gives plenty of headroom without enterprise pricing.

🟥 4. Enterprise Tier — “Enterprise”

Price: $100.00+ per month
Pricing scales based on:

Candidate volume

Position volume

Optional onboarding setup

Limits:

Unlimited active positions

Unlimited candidates
