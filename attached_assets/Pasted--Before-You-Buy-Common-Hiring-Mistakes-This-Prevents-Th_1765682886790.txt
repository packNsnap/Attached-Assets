Section 2: The Most Common Resume Blind Spots

List concrete, painful examples:

Names, emails, or companies that don’t match across sections

Career jumps that are technically possible but statistically unlikely

Skills listed without evidence of usage

Job descriptions that repeat across different employers

Timelines that only work if the candidate worked two full-time roles simultaneously

AI-written summaries that say everything and prove nothing
